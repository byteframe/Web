Proper cooling is paramount to the successful operating of a computer. In terms of overclocking, when you increase voltage to ensure more power for sucessful component operating, you increase the ammount of heat that component generates.

While many components offer stock cooling options. For successful cool, and quiet operatation of components at overclocked speeds, a powerful after market cooler is required.

Many compnaies producde innovative cooling solutions for hardware enthusiasts. The Zalman 9500 Cu LED, shown to the right, is an example of a very powerful CPU cooler, with many features. An investment in one of these will go along way to getting the most out of your computer system.

High powered cpu coolers also exist for the GPU, RAM, NorthBridge, and other hot spots in your computer.
